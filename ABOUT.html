HOME WORKS ABOUT CONTACT

It's Alex and I studied a Superior Grade in 3D Animation and Interactive Environments. My purpose is to focus on creating concept vehicles, animal sculpting as well as creating and adapting its respective rig.

I am passionate about tools for vector drawing, video editing, image editing, and especially 3D modeling. My goal is to refine every detail to the highest standard within my current knowledge while continually expanding my expertise by learning new programs and techniques.

My primary focus is the creation of a wide variety of vehicles and realistic models, despite having worked on cartoon-style projects. These experiences have reinforced my belief that what began as a hobby could one day evolve into my future career, much like the journey of many professionals in this field who share the same passion.
