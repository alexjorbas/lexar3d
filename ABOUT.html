It's Alex and I studied a Superior Grade on 3D Animation and Interactive Environments. My purpose is to focus on creating concept vehicles, animal sculpting as well as creating and adapting its respective rig.

I am passionate about all the tools related to vector drawing, video editing, image editing and especially 3D modeling. My aim is to polish every detail as much as possible in order to achieve the best results within my knowledge and to consistently learn how to cover more programs and techniques.

My focus is much related on the creation of a wide variety of vehicles as well as realistic models, despite having worked on some cartoon projects. The programs which I primarily use are Maya, After Effects, Substance Painter, Marmoset ToolBag4, Nuke Foundry, ZBrush, Photoshop and Illustrator.
